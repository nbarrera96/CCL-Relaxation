CCL Relaxtion Room

A space designed with your productivity and well-being in mind, providing students and staff with serene spaces to recharge. Color coded labs Relaxtion room is the perfect place for students to study without distracttions or for those looking to wind down. Relaxation rooms are designed to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and encourage peacefulness. By providing a calm and tranquil environment, relaxation rooms can offer numerous benefits to individuals who use them. The benefits of relaxation rooms include reduced stress, improved mental health, increased productivity, better physical health, enhanced creativity, and improved social connections. These benefits can be particularly important in workplaces, educational settings, and healthcare facilities, where stress and tension can be high. By taking a break in a relaxation room, individuals can recharge, refocus, and return to their work or daily tasks with renewed energy and a clearer mind. Overall, relaxation rooms can provide a safe and calming space where individuals can unwind and improve their mental and physical well-being.

Our Relaxtion Room features:

  • Soft lighting and comfortable seating
  • Aromatherapy: Essential oils and candles can provide a calming aroma that can enhance relaxation.
  • White noise with optimal acoustics: Soft music or white noise can help mask distracting sounds and promote relaxation.
  • Plants:Plants can help purify the air and add a natural, calming touch to the space.
  • Massage Chairs:Bolsters, blocks, straps, and blankets can help support the body in various meditation or yoga postures.
  • Waterfall Aquarium:Provides many benefits to both your physical and mental health including stress and anxiety reduction.
  • Massage Chairs:Can help alleviate pain and discomfort in the back, neck, shoulders, and legs. The gentle pressure and kneading of the muscles can stimulate the production of endorphins, which are the body's natural painkillers and mood enhancers.
